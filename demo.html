What Makes Wem Different
1
🛡️
8-Layer Safety System
Every Wem ride includes dash cam recording, live route monitoring, one-tap SOS, Claude AI check-ins, car verification, trusted contact sharing, and our unique Guest Safety Link — no other DFW rideshare offers all of this.
SAFETY FIRST
2
✦
Claude AI Built In
Riders can book conversationally — just tell Claude where you want to go. Claude also monitors rides in real time, sends mid-ride check-ins, and provides instant support. Powered by Anthropic.
POWERED BY ANTHROPIC
3
🔗
Guest Safety Link
Book a ride for someone who doesn't have the app — they receive a secure SMS link with live tracking, car verification, SOS, and Claude check-ins. The link expires automatically when the ride ends. No app download needed.
UNIQUE TO WEM
4
💰
Drivers Earn More
75% base pay in Year 1, increasing to 78% in Year 2. Gas adjustment ($0.15/mi) and tips go 100% to drivers. Pickup compensation for longer pickups. The most driver-favorable pay structure in DFW.
HIGHEST PAY IN DFW
5
⚡
Wem Pass Subscription
$14.99/month eliminates surge pricing, gas adjustment fees, and pickup fees. Riders save significantly on frequent rides while drivers still receive full compensation — Wem absorbs the difference.
$14.99 / MONTH
6
👩
Female Driver Option
Female riders can request a female driver — verified through additional background checks and training. A safety feature no major rideshare platform currently offers in DFW.
UNIQUE TO WEM
